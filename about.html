About Me

My Name is Chris Eastman and I created Net-Head.ca.

For years now I have been studying everything computer related, I have graduated Web Animation and Design and am currently taking Computer Programming, both 2-year Programs at Georgian College in Barrie, Ontario. I have always loved working with technology and using the Internet to discover new things.

My main goal in the industry is to make a name for myself as a Web and Desktop developer. My Dream Job would be working on high-end video games, whether designing characters and environments or coding the logic and functionality itself. I am a self proclaimed video-game enthusiast and love to play almost any game out there!

Net-Head is meant to get my name some web presence, and showcase the skills I have accumulated over my years of being an information technology Student.
